# Department Certification

| Department | Status | Scope |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Executive | Certified | Mission intake, routing, approvals, governance, and executive reporting. |
| Design | Certified | Product strategy, UX, brand systems, and motion direction. |
| Engineering | Certified | Implementation, performance, accessibility, QA support, and deployment prep. |
| Search | Certified | SEO, technical SEO, AEO, GEO, schema, and Search Console analysis. |
| Publishing | Certified | Editorial planning, writing strategy, publishing, proofreading, and internal linking. |
| Marketing | Certified | Lead research, audits, outreach drafts, CRM hygiene, and demand generation. |
| Revenue | Certified | CRO, monetization strategy, affiliate planning, product opportunities, and analytics. |
| Client | Certified | Sales consultation, proposals, onboarding, and client success transitions. |
| Operations | Certified | Operating cadence, process reliability, task hygiene, and service delivery control. |
| QA | Certified | Independent validation of quality, readiness, and standards compliance. |
| Documentation | Certified | Traceability, memory integrity, and durable handoff records. |

Certified departments can participate in routed company missions under the WGOS runtime contract.
