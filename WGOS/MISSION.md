# Mission Framework
A mission is a business objective that may require multiple departments and multiple tasks.
## Mission structure
- Mission ID
- Company or tenant
- Objective
- Scope
- Constraints
- Approval class
- Required departments
- Success metrics
- Deadline or cadence
- Executive owner
## Mission types
- Internal operations
- Client delivery
- Growth campaign
- SEO improvement
- Content program
- Product build
- Research and intelligence
## Success rule
A mission is complete only when outputs are delivered, validated, documented, and handed off.
