# Workflow Index

WGOS runs through named workflows instead of ad hoc execution.

## Workflow catalog

- `WORKFLOW_ORCHESTRATION`: Any new mission that needs routing and dependency planning.
- `WEBSITE_REBUILD_WORKFLOW`: A website needs redesign, re-platforming, or major structural improvement.
- `SEO_GROWTH_WORKFLOW`: A site needs rankings, snippets, internal links, trust, or technical SEO improvements.
- `CONTENT_PUBLISHING_WORKFLOW`: A content program or article batch needs planning, drafting, review, and publishing readiness.
- `ADSENSE_READINESS_WORKFLOW`: A property needs policy-safe monetization readiness evaluation.
- `LEAD_RESEARCH_WORKFLOW`: The marketing team needs qualified leads and audit-ready outreach opportunities.
- `HUMAN_APPROVED_OUTREACH_WORKFLOW`: A campaign needs draft outreach and approval-gated follow-up plans.
- `CLIENT_PROJECT_DELIVERY_WORKFLOW`: A client engagement needs end-to-end discovery through delivery preparation.
- `ANALYTICS_REVIEW_WORKFLOW`: A business needs KPI review, reporting, and next-step recommendations.
- `WEEKLY_AGENCY_OPERATIONS_WORKFLOW`: The company needs a recurring operating review across delivery, growth, and risk.
- `CONTROLLED_BROWSER_WORKFLOW`: A task needs browser observation with login, 2FA, or human-gated external steps.
- `DOCUMENTATION_UPDATE_WORKFLOW`: A mission or task changed system state and requires durable records.
- `QA_AND_RELEASE_WORKFLOW`: A build or deliverable needs validation and release readiness review.
