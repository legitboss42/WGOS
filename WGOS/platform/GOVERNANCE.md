# Platform Governance

Document platform ownership, company isolation, approval routing, audit logging, version history, and documentation policy.

## Governance rules

- Shared runtime may be reused across companies, but tenant data may not be merged.
- Rule Zero applies at both platform and company levels.
- Every approval, report, and mission record must remain traceable to a company or shared-service owner.
- Cross-company playbooks must remove confidential client details before promotion.
