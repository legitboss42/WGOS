# Permission Model

## Roles

- Founder or Owner: full company and platform oversight.
- Executive operators: mission routing, KPI review, approval coordination.
- Department managers: company-scoped planning and specialist coordination.
- Specialists: task execution inside approved tenant boundaries.
- QA, Knowledge, and Documentation: review, archive, and reporting authority without cross-tenant data export.

## Rules

- Default access is least privilege.
- Company memory, CRM, reports, and approvals are tenant-scoped by default.
- Global reporting may expose aggregate counts and de-identified benchmarks only.
- Approval routing follows company ownership before platform ownership when both apply.
- Secret handling, credential use, and external system actions always require explicit approval rules defined in WGOS.
