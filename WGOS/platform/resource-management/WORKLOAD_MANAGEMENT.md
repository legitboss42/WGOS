# Workload Management

Track agent workload, department workload, mission queues, execution history, active integrations, and pending approvals across companies.

## Scheduling rules

- Prioritize owner-approved revenue and delivery missions first.
- Do not over-assign specialists across concurrent company missions without QA and documentation capacity.
- Pause new work when approval queues or QA queues exceed the operating threshold defined by executive review.
- Rebalance work by department before reassigning tenant ownership.
